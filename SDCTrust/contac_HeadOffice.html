Vision

To become an institution par excellence, providing comprehensive habilitation measures and pioneering efforts for enriching the lives of challenged individuals.

Mission

  • To help every challenged child by Reaching medical & therapeutic assistance Imparting academic training appropriate to specific needs Equipping with appropriate Vocational skills Creating necessary social and physical support infrastructure that empowers the child to lead a life of independence and dignity in adulthood.
