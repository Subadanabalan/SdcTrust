Vision

To support the youth in promoting self-confidence helping them discover themselves to make the right choice in life and thus become self-reliant and dynamic.

Mission

  • To engage undeserved young people through Community-based education.
  • To mentor & organise employment programmes to help the developing skills.
  • To provide high quality & culturally competent services to the deserving youth.
